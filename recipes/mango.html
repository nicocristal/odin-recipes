Mango Dessert

INGREDIENTS

  • 120g Biscuits
  • 25g Melted butter
  • 1 Mango
  • 250g Mascarpone
  • 50g Sugar
  • 75ml Heavy cream
  • 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract

STEPS

  1. To make the biscuit base, finely crush the biscuits by putting into a sealed plastic bag and bashing with a rolling pin (alternatively, pulse to crumbs using a food processor)
  2. Pour the melted butter over the biscuit crumbs and mix, until thoroughly combined
  3. Put the crumb mixture into a mold and press down evenly with your fingers or using a spoon
  4. Cut the mango into small cubes
  5. Blend the mango cubes using an immersion blender until smooth
  6. In a bowl, put 250g Mascarpone
  7. Add 50g Sugar
  8. Add 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  9. Mix well, then add 75ml Heavy cream, and mix well again
  10. Spread the mascarpone cream on the surface of the biscuits
  11. Pour the Mango coulis on top
  12. (Optional) Refrigerate it until desired temperature
