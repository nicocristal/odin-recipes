Mango Dessert

This is one of my favorite breakfasts - it's easy, filling, and relatively healthy. Hope you try it and enjoy!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/3 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 tsp Everything But the Bagel Seasoning
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 pieces of bread of choosing

STEPS

  1. Scoop out half an avocado and mash in a bowl with a fork. Mix in the cottage cheese and Everything But the Bagel Seasoning.
  2. Cook your eggs. (My technique: Grease a cast-iron skillet and turn it on medium-high heat for a few minutes. Crack room-temperature eggs into the hot skillet and add salt and pepper. Cover eggs with a pan lid and cook for about 3-4 minutes, just until the yolks change color. Remove eggs from heat.)
  3. Toast your bread and top the toast with the avocado mixture, then place a sunny-side up egg on each piece of toast.
  4. Accept that you have eaten avocado toast and now will never be able to afford to buy a home.
